Top Features

Compressor & Limiter

Advanced real-time compression that carefully controls track dynamics to prevent clipping and balance volume without losing clarity.

Auto-Gain

Intelligent normalization that equalizes perceived loudness across different tracks and genres automatically.

Smart Detection

Constantly monitors the audio signal to detect if a song is too compressed or too dynamic on the fly.

EQ & Highpass

A powerful collection of 16 precision EQ presets combined with a dedicated highpass filter to remove unwanted sub-bass rumble and noise.

How to Install

1

Download

Download the latest .zip from GitHub Releases.

2

Load Unpacked

In Chrome, go to Extensions, enable Developer Mode, then click Load unpacked.

3

Play Music

Open YouTube Music and click the K-ROOM icon in the player bar.
